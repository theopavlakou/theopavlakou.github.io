I am a PhD student at the University of Edinburgh in the Informatics Department under the supervision of Dr. Iain Murray. I am interested in Machine Learning and my particular area of research is in Density Estimation.
Before starting my PhD, I did a Masters by Research in Data Science (as part of the CDT in Data Science) and the title of my thesis was Large Scale Optimisation Algorithms for Machine Learning. This was supervised by Dr. Peter Richtarik.
Prior to that, I did my undergraduate degree at Imperial College London where I studied Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
